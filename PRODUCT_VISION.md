# AFOS Studio – Product Vision

## What is AFOS?

AFOS (Autonomous Filmmaking Operating System) is an AI-native production platform that manages the complete filmmaking process from story to final production package.

AFOS is not a chatbot.

AFOS is not a prompt library.

AFOS is an operating system that coordinates specialized AI departments to produce professional-quality films.

---

## Vision

Enable anyone to create cinematic films with the quality and organization of a professional film studio.

---

## Mission

Transform an idea, screenplay, PDF, or story into a complete production-ready filmmaking package through a guided workflow.

---

## Who is AFOS for?

* Beginner creators
* YouTube filmmakers
* Indie filmmakers
* Animation studios
* AI content creators
* Professional production companies

---

## Core Principles

1. Guided workflow
2. Production-first thinking
3. Visual consistency
4. Character continuity
5. Camera continuity
6. Environment continuity
7. AI collaboration between specialized departments
8. Professional cinematic quality

---

## Long-Term Goal

Become the world's leading AI-powered filmmaking operating system.
